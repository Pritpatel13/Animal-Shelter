Hello, I am a Star Tortoise.

I do not belong here. My ancestors lived in the wild, but many of us are taken away by people driven by greed and profit. We are captured, trafficked, and sold illegally as if we are nothing more than objects.

During transportation, we are often crammed into plastic bottles, cans, and other terrible containers. There is barely any room to move or breathe. Many of my brothers and sisters do not survive the journey. [pauses] But the smugglers rarely care. To them, we are only a source of money.

Many people believe that keeping a tortoise at home brings luck, prosperity, and wealth. [firmly] This is simply a myth. There is no truth in it. What it really brings is suffering for animals like me, who are taken away from our natural homes.

My place is in the wild, surrounded by nature, where I can explore, grow, and live freely in the environment I was born to inhabit.

Thankfully, Team Prayas rescued me and many others of my species. They provide us with safety, care, and kindness. They treat us with the respect and compassion that every living creature deserves.

Because of them, I have a second chance at life.

Now, let me ask you something...

Would you be comfortable if someone took you away from your home, your family, and everything familiar to you... and forced you to spend the rest of your life inside a plastic tub?
