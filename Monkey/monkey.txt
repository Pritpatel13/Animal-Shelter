Hello, I am a monkey.

One day, I was sitting near a road, minding my own business, when a truck driver saw me alone. Instead of letting me stay with my family and in the forest I called home, he picked me up and took me away.

I didn't understand what was happening. Everything was unfamiliar. The trees were gone. My family was gone. Day after day, I was carried from one place to another, with no way to return home.

I was lonely... frightened... and completely lost.

Then, some kind people noticed my condition and contacted Team Prayas. They came for me when I needed help the most.

Team Prayas rescued me, gave me proper food, medical care, and a safe place to recover. For the first time in a long while, I felt protected and cared for.

We monkeys are not pets. We are not meant to be taken from the wild or kept for human amusement. Our home is in the forests, among the trees, where we can live freely with our families and communities.

Every monkey that is taken from the wild loses more than its freedom. We lose our families, our homes, and the life nature intended for us.

Thank you, Team Prayas, for giving me a second chance and helping me find safety again.

Tell me... how would you feel if someone took you away from your family, your home, and everything you loved... and never let you go back?
