Hello, I am a Turkey.

You may know me for my unique "gobble, gobble" call. It often makes people smile.

But not everyone sees me as a living creature. Some people see me only as an opportunity to make money.

Many turkeys like me are captured from our natural habitats and trapped in small cages. We lose the freedom to run, explore, and live the way nature intended.

The cages are cramped and frightening. There is little space to move, and every day feels stressful and uncertain.

For young turkeys, the situation is even worse. Many are separated from their parents before they are ready to survive on their own. They are left confused, frightened, and vulnerable.

Illegal wildlife trade does more than harm individual animals. It reduces wild populations and disrupts the balance of nature.

No living creature should be taken from its home simply so someone else can make a profit.
